### Description
AgriPulse delivers real-time agricultural commodity news and market intelligence — built for Indian traders, farmers, and agribusiness professionals.

**Stay ahead of the market:**
• Breaking news for 30+ commodities — Wheat, Sugar, Paddy, Chana, Mustard, Edible Oils, Spices, and more
• Real-time updates from 50+ trusted Indian news sources — NDTV, Business Standard, Economic Times, LiveMint, and more
• India vs Global news classification — filter what matters to your market
• Equity market tracking — Indian stocks, global indices, crypto, and mutual funds

**Smart features:**
• AI-powered article summaries (Apple Intelligence)
• Commodity Calendar with 130+ agricultural events
• Save articles and export to PDF
• Share to WhatsApp and Telegram with one tap
• Push notifications for breaking commodity news

**Government & regulatory feeds:**
• PIB (Press Information Bureau) updates
• DGFT trade notifications
• IMD weather advisories & ICAR agricultural research

**Designed for professionals:**
• Dark theme optimized for extended reading
• Pagination for large article feeds
• Search across all commodities and saved articles
• Background refresh — news updates every 2 hours automatically

Free. No ads. No subscription. Built for the Indian agricultural community.
